Stuffed Bell Peppers

Colorful peppers filled with cheesy rice goodness.

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • ½ cup grated cheese
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds.
  3. Mix rice with cheese, herbs, salt & pepper.
  4. Stuff mixture into peppers and place in baking dish.
  5. Bake 25–30 minutes until peppers are tender.

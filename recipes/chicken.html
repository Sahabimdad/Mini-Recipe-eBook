Herb & Lemon Chicken

Tender chicken with fresh herbs and zesty lemon.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp mixed fresh herbs
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, herbs, salt & pepper.
  3. Coat chicken with mixture and place on baking tray.
  4. Roast 35–40 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with a side of vegetables.

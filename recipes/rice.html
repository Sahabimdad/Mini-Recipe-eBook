Quick Spiced Rice

Fluffy rice with simple warming spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice under cold water until clear.
  2. In a pot, heat a little oil and toast cumin seeds.
  3. Add rice, turmeric, and water. Bring to boil.
  4. Cover, reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve.

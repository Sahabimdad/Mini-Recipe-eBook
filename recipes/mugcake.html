Chocolate Mug Cake

Quick, single-serve chocolate cake in a mug.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a microwave-safe mug until smooth.
  2. Microwave for 60–90 seconds until cooked through.
  3. Let it cool slightly and enjoy warm.
